Sinangag

Finished sinangag recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cold, cooked white rice
  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • Lots of garlic, peeled and minced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, break cold rice to separate grains.
  2. In a wide skillet over low heat, heat about ¼ cup oil. Add garlic and cook, stirring occasionally until golden brown. With a slotted spoon, remove the garlic and drain on paper towels.
  3. Remove excess oil except for about 1 to 2 tablespoons. Raise heat to high, swirling the oil to coat the surface of the pan.
  4. Add rice and cook, spreading on the surface of the pan for about 45 seconds or until grains start to sizzle and then toss to redistribute. Repeat a few times until rice is heated through.
  5. Return ¾ of the garlic and toss with the fried rice until evenly distributed.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. To serve, transfer to a serving platter and top with the remaining garlic bits.
